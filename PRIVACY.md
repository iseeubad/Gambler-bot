# Privacy Policy — Gambler’s Showdown

**Last updated:** 2026-01-29

Gambler’s Showdown (“the Bot”) is a Discord game bot created for entertainment purposes only.  
This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

The Bot collects only the minimum data necessary for gameplay, including:

- Discord User IDs  
- Discord Server (Guild) IDs  
- Game-related data (scores, inventory items, AFK status, round progress)

No personal data such as emails, passwords, or real names is collected.

---

## 2. How Data Is Used

Collected data is used **only** to:

- Manage and run game sessions  
- Track scores, eliminations, and inventories  
- Enforce gameplay and AFK rules  

Data is **never** used for advertising or profiling.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- Data is stored temporarily or persistently depending on gameplay needs.
- No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.
- Game data may be reset when games end, the bot restarts, or for maintenance purposes.

---

## 4. Direct Messages (DMs)

The Bot may send **direct messages** to users strictly for gameplay purposes, such as:

- Item selection
- Gamble / Play Safe decisions
- AFK or elimination notifications

The Bot does not send unsolicited or promotional DMs.

---

## 5. User Control & Data Removal

- Users may stop using the Bot at any time.
- Leaving a server removes the user from all active games in that server.
- Users may request data deletion by contacting the developer through the official support server.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The Bot does not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 13.  
Use of Discord itself is subject to Discord’s Terms of Service.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.  
Continued use of the Bot after updates implies acceptance of the revised policy.

---

## 8. Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official discord profile **kujo4jotaro**.
